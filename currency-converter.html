Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the exchange rates?
Our rates are fetched from open financial data APIs and are updated regularly. They closely reflect mid-market rates. For large transactions, we recommend checking with your bank for the exact rate they offer.
How many currencies does this converter support?
Our currency converter supports 150+ world currencies including all major currencies like USD, EUR, GBP, as well as regional currencies like PKR, INR, AED, SAR and many more.
What is the mid-market exchange rate?
The mid-market rate is the midpoint between the buy and sell prices of two currencies. It's the fairest rate available and is used as a benchmark. Banks and exchange services add a markup to this rate.
How do I convert USD to PKR?
Simply enter your amount, select USD as the From currency and PKR as the To currency, then click Convert. You'll instantly see the result along with the current exchange rate.
Why do exchange rates change every day?
Exchange rates change due to factors like interest rate decisions by central banks, inflation data, trade balances, political events, and market speculation. The forex market trades 24 hours a day, 5 days a week.
Is this currency converter free to use?
Yes, our currency converter is completely free with no registration required. You can convert any amount between any two currencies as many times as you want.
